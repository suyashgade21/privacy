# Privacy Policy



## 🔒 Privacy-First Design

PDFmint Pro is built with a **privacy-first approach**. All core features work entirely on your device, ensuring your files remain under your control.

## 📁 Your Files Stay on Your Device

All PDF operations — including scanning, merging, compressing, signing, splitting, rotating, extracting, and watermarking — are performed locally on your device.
We do **not upload, store, or access your files** on any external servers.

## 📷 Camera Permission

Camera access is used only when you choose to scan documents.
Captured images are processed instantly on your device and are never shared externally.

## 💾 Storage Access

Storage permission is used only to save and open PDF files created within the app.
We do not access or scan unrelated files on your device.

## 📊 Minimal Data Collection

PDFmint Pro itself does **not collect or store any personal information**, such as your name, email, or precise location.
No account or sign-in is required.

## 📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

To support free usage, the app displays ads using **Google AdMob**, a service provided by Google.

AdMob may automatically collect **limited, non-personal data**, such as:

* Device type and OS version
* Advertising ID
* Approximate location (for general ad relevance)
* Basic app interaction data

This data is used only to **display and improve ads**.
PDFmint Pro does not control or store this data.

## 🔐 Your Data, Your Control

* Your files never leave your device
* No file content is shared with any third party
* No personal data is stored by the app

## 🚫 No Selling of Data

We do **not sell, rent, or trade any user data**.

## 👶 Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 🔄 Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or regulatory changes. Updates will be posted with a revised date.
